## Landscape Services In

## Centreville

Centreville's character comes from its older established subdivisions and its surrounding open spaces along Bull Run — and that combination shapes how we design landscapes here. Working across Sully Station, Bull Run, Faircrest, Virginia Run, and Little Rocky Run, Sunrise Landscape & Design has helped Centreville homeowners transform aging landscapes into outdoor spaces that match the rest of how they've invested in their homes. Many Centreville properties were built in the 1980s and 1990s, which means original landscape designs are now decades old, foundations have settled, and drainage issues have often emerged over time. We specialize in landscape renovations: tearing out tired plantings, retrofitting drainage and grading, building hardscape patios where lawns used to be, and installing modern lighting and irrigation that brings older properties up to today's standard. Whether you're a long-time Centreville resident planning a backyard refresh or a newer homeowner ready to start from scratch, our team is here to help.
