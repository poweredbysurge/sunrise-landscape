## Landscape Services In

## Reston

Reston was built around a planned-community vision that puts trees, trails, and natural space at the center — and that vision still shapes the landscape work that matters most here. Across Lake Anne, North Point, South Lakes, Hunters Woods, and Tall Oaks, Sunrise Landscape & Design has helped Reston homeowners create outdoor spaces that complement the community's mature tree canopy rather than fight it. Reston's older properties often come with shaded lots, root-bound soils, and dated hardscape that needs careful renovation. Our team specializes in native and shade-adapted plantings, drainage retrofits for properties that have seen decades of seasonal flooding, and modern hardscape design that respects the area's mid-century roots while meeting today's expectations. Whether you're designing a low-maintenance front yard near Lake Anne, installing a patio in Hunters Woods, or putting your townhome on a sustainable landscape maintenance program, our work in Reston is shaped by a real understanding of what this community values.
