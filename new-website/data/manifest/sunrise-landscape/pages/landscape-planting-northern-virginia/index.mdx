# Landscape Planting Services in Northern Virginia

We believe a great planting does more than fill space — it brings life, texture, and story to your landscape. With an artist’s eye and decades of experience, we create living environments that evolve beautifully with time and purpose.

## What We Offer

### Custom Garden and Planting Design

We create thoughtful planting layouts using trees, shrubs, perennials, annuals, and ground covers. Each design balances color, texture, and seasonal interest to keep your landscape vibrant year-round.

### Specialty Garden Design and Installation

We design specialty gardens that reflect your unique style and site conditions, including native, English, woodland, wetland, shade, and meadow gardens. These gardens are crafted to thrive with minimal upkeep while supporting local wildlife and pollinators.

### Turf & Lawn Installation

Our team installs high-quality turf and lawn systems for a lush, durable finish. From expert soil preparation to the final roll of sod, we handle every detail with care. You will enjoy a smooth, healthy lawn that enhances your entire landscape.

### Planters & Seasonal Displays

We create stunning planters and fresh seasonal displays to add color and charm to your property. Each arrangement is designed to highlight the best of every season with carefully selected plants and flowers.

### Year-Round Installations

We plan and schedule plantings at the optimal time for each species to ensure strong roots and long-term success. This approach means your landscape stays healthy and beautiful no matter the season. Trust our team to handle every detail for year-round results.

## Our Landscape Planting Process

Our designs consider:

### 1. Consultation

We start with a conversation about your goals

### 2. Site Visit

A designer walks the property with you to assess layout, sunlight, soil, and use

### 3. Concept Design

You’ll receive initial ideas and ballpark pricing

### 4. Design Refinement

Together, we fine-tune materials, plants, and placement

### 5. Installation & Care

We bring your design to life and support it to thrive
